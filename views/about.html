About

Everything you want to know about me - the designer & developer.


"I am Levi Ackerman, a symbol of unwavering determination and unmatched prowess, echoing the relentless spirit of humanity within the gripping saga of 'Attack on Titan'."

Levi Ackerman, a character etched into the annals of anime history, is the epitome of unwavering determination and unparalleled skill in the gripping world of "Attack on Titan." In the dystopian universe where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to colossal humanoid creatures known as Titans, Levi emerges as a beacon of hope and a symbol of resilience. His prowess in combat is legendary, unmatched by any other soldier within the walls. With lightning-fast reflexes and an unyielding resolve, Levi fearlessly confronts Titans, demonstrating a finesse that borders on the supernatural. Beneath his stoic facade lies a profound sense of responsibility, not just for his own survival, but for the safety of his fellow soldiers and the remnants of humanity. Levi's leadership is magnetic; his decisions are calculated and precise.
